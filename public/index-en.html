It is our great pleasure to welcome you to our villa, lovingly designed with passion and care. We sincerely hope your stay will be delightful and bring you as much joy as it does to us.

Take your time to slip into the Caribbean rhythm and discover the many treasures of this exceptional island. Whether you love hiking (to spectacular waterfalls), scuba diving, relaxing on paradise beaches such as Malendure or Anse de la Perle, or sharing convivial moments over a drink (the local rum is worth a proper tasting!), we wish you an unforgettable stay.
Explore dream beaches (Malendure, Anse Leroux, Anse de la Perle…), set off on memorable hikes (Chutes du Carbet, La Lézarde, Trou à Diable…), or discover the underwater world with nearby dive clubs like Les Heures Saines.

📋 Villa description

Contemporary villa of 180 m² on a 1,350 m² plot, ideal for up to 6 guests. It features 3 bedrooms of 15 m² each with their own en‑suite bathroom, an equipped kitchen, an indoor lounge and a large outdoor terrace of 50 m² with a private pool, covered dining area, outdoor lounge, parking for 5 cars, as well as autonomous amenities such as two water reserves (rainwater and mains) and a solar water heater.
